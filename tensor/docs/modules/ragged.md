## ragged

Packed ragged sequence utilities and segmented reductions.

### APIs
- `pack_sequences`, `unpack_sequences`
- Segment ops: `segment_sum` (`ragged_segment_sum`), `segment_mean`, `segment_max`
- Packed reductions: `ragged_packed_softmax`, `ragged_packed_logsumexp`
- Scans: `ragged_inclusive_cumsum`, `ragged_exclusive_cumsum`
